Lockstep Realty — KPI Dashboard

Interactive filters (date + agent). Data auto-refreshes from your Google Sheet CSV.
Tip: After you paste it once, it saves in your browser (localStorage).
You must publish your sheet as CSV (File → Share → Publish to the web → CSV). This dashboard expects at minimum: Date, Agent, and either Agent Income or GCI/Commission. It will try to “guess” the right columns by name.
